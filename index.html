Our Story

Family-built, community-trusted.

Founded in 2005, our family-owned company has built over 200 homes across Greater Vancouver. Many of our clients return to build their second or third home — that kind of trust means everything to us.

We've maintained strong relationships with the same trusted subcontractors for two decades, ensuring consistently high-quality work. Led personally by Cheema and family, customer care is at the core of everything we do.

Vancouver's booming housing market has brought its share of challenges — abandoned projects and poor craftsmanship from inexperienced builders undercutting each other. At C-Best, we take pride in our honesty, integrity, and long-standing reputation. We specialize in building 1, 2, and 3 story houses, townhouses, and laneway homes.
